EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
OrbiSave is a digital financial infrastructure platform that modernizes Africa's traditional community savings groups (chamas, VSLAs, cooperatives) through automated mobile money integration, immutable digital ledgers, and regulated bank custody—while preserving the essential social governance structures that make these groups work.
The Core Insight
600M+ smallholder farmers across sub-Saharan Africa rely on informal savings groups as their primary financial institution. These groups manage $38B annually but operate on paper ledgers, cash handling, and vulnerable trust-based systems. No existing solution has successfully combined group governance, seasonal savings scheduling, automated mobile money, and regulated fund custody.

Key Structural Problems
1. Seasonal Cash Flow Mismatch: Farmers earn in 2-3 harvest windows annually but need working capital year-round. Existing financial products demand fixed monthly contributions—structurally incompatible with agricultural income patterns.
2. No Safe Savings Infrastructure: Banks require documentation, collateral, minimum balances, and physical branches. 75% of rural Kenyans are excluded from formal banking. Mobile wallets (M-Pesa) offer convenience but zero savings discipline.
3. Paper-Based Group Vulnerability: Traditional chamas rely on manual cash collection, paper ledgers prone to errors/disputes/fraud, single treasurers holding all cash, and no verifiable financial history for future credit access.
4. Financial Shock Destruction: Without savings buffers, farmers remain one illness, drought, or equipment failure away from destitution—permanently trapped in poverty cycles.

2.2 Core Value Propositions
For Smallholder Farmers
1. Built for Seasonal Income: Contribution schedules adapt to harvest cycles—not fixed monthly debits. Farmers save during abundance, draw down during lean seasons.
2. Preserves Chama Culture: The community savings group is upgraded, not replaced. Members still gather, vote, and govern together.
3. Builds Financial Identity: Consistent savings history creates verifiable financial records—the bridge to formal credit.
4. Protects Against Shocks: Group loan pools enable emergency fund access within 48 hours—without predatory lenders.
5. Universal Accessibility: USSD-based STK push works on any mobile phone—no smartphone required.
6. Compounding Growth: Automated rotation payouts, consistent contributions, and internal loan interest returned to the group create tangible wealth accumulation.
For Urban and Informal Workers
Value Dimension
OrbiSave Advantage
DISCIPLINE
M-Pesa is too easy to spend. OrbiSave locks contributions into group pools with social accountability—peers see your record, creating behavioral discipline solo products cannot replicate.
SECURITY
Unlike treasurers holding cash, funds sit in regulated bank trust accounts. No single member can access pools unilaterally. Every transaction is logged, timestamped, and visible in real time.
GROWTH
Saving KES 500/week builds rotation payouts, accesses internal credit at fair rates, and after 12 months creates a financial track record. SACCOs take months to join; OrbiSave works immediately.
